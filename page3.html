Things to do

No doubts that the great majority of people come to Cayo Largo for the beauty and the tranquillity of its beaches and almost virgin nature. However, there are also many activities and excursions available in the island. The all-inclusive packages offers you the opportunity to enjoy all the activities listed below. It is important to note that certain activities require a day or more in advance booking.


WALKING
Cayo Largo is truly a walker's paradise due to its 20km of paradisiac beaches all along the south coastline. It is possible to walk hours towards East to Playa Blanca, Playa Los Cocos and Playa Tortuga or west to Playa Paraiso and Playa Sirena. Walking is made easy by the fine white sand, which never gets too hot. The beaches east from the hotels are usually narrower while the ones west of the resorts are generally wider.

CYCLING
With the all-inclusive package you can also use bicycles for one hours per day per person. It is not recommended to cycle to the west beaches due to their sandy trails.

WATER SPORTS
Cayo Largo offers a wide variety of water sports. The all-inclusive package usually include one hour per day per person of the use of non-motorized equipment such as kayak, pedal boat, snorkelling gear, etc. The water sport equipment are located in Playa Sirena. You may also find other water sports such as scuba diving at the marina.

SPORTS
Volleyball: You will usually find volleyball net on the beach at Sol Pelicano; Isla der Sur and Sol Cayo Largo. Some hotels also offer pool volleyball.
Tennis: You will find ennis courts at Sol Pelicano, Isla del Sur, Sol Cayo Largo and Ole Playa Blanca hotels. The all-inclusive package allows you to use them one hour per day per person.

AEROBICS
Everyday aerobics session are given at the major hotels usually near the pool or the beach.

DAILY ACTIVITIES AND NIGHT ENTERTAINMENT
Everyday many games and sports are offered by the Entertainment staff. At night shows, music and dancing are available.

FIESTA
Party with entertainment team every Friday in the village. 4 CUC per person. Ask your hotel for availability.

 

About The Island

BEACHES
Cayo Largo is home of some of the most beautiful beaches in the world. The south coastline is a stretch of more than 20 km of fine white sand and crystal-clear turquoise waters.
There is no Clothing Optional beach in Playa Sirena.
The most crowded beach is in front of the hotels Sol Cayo Largo, Sol Pelicano and Ole Playa Blanca and at Playa Sirena when, regularly, excursions from the main island of Cuba come to spend the day.
From the Sol Cayo Largo to the West beaches and East beaches of the hotel Ole Playa Blanca you will find virgin beaches just for yourself, pelicans and other birds, iguanas, turtles, crabs, etc.

FLORA
Coastal vegetation with predominance of: jack beans (Caesalpinia bonduc), purslane (Sesuvium portulacastrum), Chilean firebush (Embothrium coccineum), coco plum trees (Chrysobalanus icaco), bay cedar (Suriana maritima), Florida thatch palms (Thrinax radiata) and other tropical coastal shrubbery. Climbing plants, sage and mangrove both adorn and protect the old dunes.

FAUNA
Multiple species including pelicans, sea gulls, iguanas, turtles, cormorants, kingbirds, mockingbirds, hummingbirds and herons.
